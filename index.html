A special place to me...

This image above is a photo of Combloux, a small mountain village located in the French Alps. I grew up learning to ski here at the ripe old age of 6. My first ski solo exploration ended with me in a pile of snow, blood all over my face, and a swift trip down the mountain on an emergency sled - which was awful due to my distinct lack of French speaking ability.
